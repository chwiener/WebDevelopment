Brooklyn Nine-Nine is an ensemble workplace comedy surrounding a New York police precinct and it's detectives. It has eight seasons, and it highly acclaimed for its humor, comedy, and wide range of topics covered.

Awards

2 Emmys, 2 Golden Globes, 2 Critics Choice Awards, 1 American Comedy Award, 1 GLAAD Media Award, 2 Imagen Foundation Awards, 3 Gracie Allen Awards, 2 OFTA Awards, 1 INOCA Award,

This show ran from 2013-2021
